Gases In Atmosphere

Earth's atmosphere is made up of:

  1. 78%: Nitrogen
  2. 21%: Oxygen
  3. 0.9%: Argon
  4. 0.1%: Other gases, including...
    • carbon dioxide
    • water vapour
    • methane
    • Neon

The atmosphere has seven layers:

  • Troposphere
  • Stratosphere
  • Mesosphere
  • Thermosphere
  • Ionosphere
  • Exosphere
  • Magnetosphere

Definitions Of Layers:

Troposphere :
The lowest layer, where all weather changes take place.
Stratosphere :
Extends from the troposphere to about 50 kilometers above Earth's surface.
Mesosphere :
The region above the stratosphere.
Thermosphere :
The thickest layer, where only the lightest gases are found.
Ionosphere :
A series of regions in the mesosphere and thermosphere where solar radiation ionizes the atmosphere.
